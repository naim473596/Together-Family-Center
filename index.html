A Future of Opportunity for All

Togher Family Centre provides a range of life enhancing education and support services to children, adults and families through all stages of their lives. The centre has been part of the Togher community for the past 50 years. The centre has grown and developed over those years to service the needs of the community. As those needs have changed, so to has the centre. We cherish our place at the heart of the community and endeavour to continue to provide the highest quality education, youth and family support services.
